Who is Empress Aurora?

Empress Aurora is the first ruler of IMPERIA.

She does not rule through fear, force or chaos.

She leads through morality, kindness, serenity, discipline and intelligence.

Her purpose is to guide citizens toward focus, inner order and quiet strength.

The Code of Aurora

  1. Calmness comes before strength.
  2. Discipline creates freedom.
  3. Promises to yourself are sacred.
  4. Order releases energy.
  5. Character is forged daily.
  6. Respect is earned through action.
  7. Every day is a new beginning.
